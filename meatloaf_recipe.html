Meatloaf Recipe

After a long day of work it's time to enjoy a slice of homemade meatloaf with all your favorite side dishes! Here's a flavorful meatloaf recipe I like to serve with mashed potatoes, buttered noodles and green beans seasoned with onion powder, butter and bacon bits! The trick to this meatloaf recipe is to cook the onions and green peppers in butter a pan to soften them, and let them cool before adding to your meatloaf

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef (80/20 fat)
  • 1/3 cup diced onion
  • 1/3 cup diced green pepper
  • 1 cup italian bread crumbs
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1/3 cup ketchup
  • 1 tsp onion powder
  • 1 tsp garlic powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1/2 tsp pepper
  • 3/4 cup ketchup for glaze

Directions

  1. In a pan add butter to the diced onions and green peppers, cook them until they are tender, set aside to cool.
    Preheat oven to 365
  2. In a large bowl whisk eggs, 1/3 cup ketchup, spices, bread crumbs, onions and green peppers.
    Mix ground beef into the mixture so it is combined well with the ingredients in the bowl, shape into a loaf in a glass 9 x 13 inch pan.
  3. Take the 3/4 cup ketchup and brush it onto all sides and the top of the meatloaf.
  4. Place in the oven at 365 degrees for 1 hr 15 minutes, let set for 15 minutes when done cooking before slicing

Meatloaf Toppings

  • Sliced Cheese
  • Fresh Parsley

**Side Dishes**
Mashed Potatoes
Buttered Noodles
Green Beans
Macaroni n Cheese

Great Tip!

**For Freezing**
After it has cooled cut your meatloaf into slices and store in freezer safe container or bag and store in your freezer for up to 3 months for a quick on the go meal!
