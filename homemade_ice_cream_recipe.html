Strawberry
Ice Cream Recipe

Mmmm my favorite meal of the day! Time for a quick and delicious strawberry ice cream dessert recipe! I have found this strawberry ice cream recipe that uses sweetened condensed milk and heavy whipping cream creates a wonderfully smooth texture that you will fall in love with!

Ingredients

  • 14 oz can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 2 cups cold heavy whipping cream
  • 1 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 can of strawberry pie filling

Directions

  1. Place the 2 colds of heavy whipping cream in a bowl, use a electric mixer to whip the cream until it has thickened and forms soft peaks and the cream sticks to the mixer blades.
  2. Pour the sweetened condensed milk and vanilla extract into a bowl and mix.
  3. Gently fold in the sweetened condensed milk and the vanilla extract mixture into the whipping cream until smooth.
  4. Pour half of the whipping cream mixture into a freezer safe container, pour half the can of strawberry pie filling over the whipping cream and swirl the two together with a butter knife.
  5. Now pour the rest of the whipping cream mixture over the strawberry cream mixture, top with the rest of the strawberry pie filling and swirl with a butter knife again
  6. Place the strawberry ice cream mixture in the freezer for at least 5 hours before serving.

Ice Cream Toppings

  • Hot Fudge
  • Caramel
  • Fresh Fruit
  • Whipped Cream

**Ice Cream Variations**
Pie Filling ~ Blueberry, Apple, Cherry
Chocolate Chips
Chocolate Syrup
Caramel Syrup
Candy Bar pieces
Mint Chocolate chips w/ mint extract

Great Tip!
Leave out the strawberries in this recipe and you have vanilla flavor homemade ice cream!!
