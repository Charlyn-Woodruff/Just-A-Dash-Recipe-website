Ground Beef
Taco Recipe

Lunchtime is a good time to recharge and enjoy some Tacos! I love a quick and delicious taco lunch recipe that the whole family will enjoy! This taco recipe is easy and you can add or omit seasoning spices based on your own preferences. I usually like to add onion powder and a little garlic powder to ours but you can spice it up however you like!

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • Soft or hard shell Taco shells
  • Mild Cheddar Shredded cheese (or your favorite cheese topping)
  • Shredded Lettuce
  • Diced Tomatoes
  • Diced Onions
  • Taco seasoning packet for the hamburger (or your own seasoning spices)

Directions

  1. Dice, chop and shred your tomatoes, lettuce and onions, set them aside in containers until you are ready to serve your tacos.
  2. Heat a large skillet over medium-high heat, add the ground beef (add your own spices if not using a taco seasoning packet) chopping the meat with a spoon or spatula into smaller pieces. Cook meat until fully done.
  3. Drain off any excess grease after browning the taco meat.
  4. If using a taco seasoning packet, add it to the cooked hamburger and follow the instructions on the packet
  5. Once you have finished cooking the taco meat with the spices, it is now ready to be added to your favorite taco shell and topped with cheese, lettuce, tomatoes, onions, and all your favorite toppings to enjoy!

Taco Toppings

  • Sour Cream
  • Ranch Dressing
  • Jalapenos
  • Salsa
  • Taco Sauce
